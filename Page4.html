SNCS LEVEL 1

Course Description

The Singapore National Climbing Standards (SNCS) Level 1 course is an introduction to sport climbing that will culminate in an assessment and Level 1 certification, sanctioned by the Singapore Mountaineering Federation (SMF).

The minimum age to take Level 1 course is 13 years old (by date of birth). This course is designed for beginners with little to no prior knowledge of sport climbing and will be undertaken by a SMF certified instructor.

During Phase 2, we will be following SMF guidelines when running the course. The theory portion of the course will be taught via a video link sent to you whilst the practical session will be completed within 3.5 hours at the facility. You must view the video before heading down to the gym.

During the 3.5 hours time at our gym, you will learn and practise the practical syllabus of the course such as tying the figure 8 knot, belaying skill, climbing and much more. At the end of the course, you will be assessed and participants who pass will be awarded the SNCS Sport Climbing Level 1 certificate. ​

Course Highlights
  • Introduction to the Sport of Climbing
  • Introduction of Climbing Equipment
  • Tie-in Knot
  • Belaying Technique (5 steps belay)
  • Basic Climbing Technique
  • Top-rope Climbing
Ideal for
  • Beginner Climbers
  • Climbers without SNCS Level 1 certification
  • *Minimum age is 13 years old (by date of birth)
